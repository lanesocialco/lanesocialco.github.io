The story

Where this
all came from.

Las Vegas · Social Strategy · Digital Products

It started with a frustration. I watched talented creatives pour time and energy into social media, do everything they thought they should, and still get nothing back. Not because they weren't trying—they were. The problem was that nobody had ever sat them down and been honest about what actually works.

The entertainment industry has a lot of noise—agents who don't answer emails, managers who over-promise, coaches selling outdated advice. Meanwhile, the platforms keep changing their rules. Most creatives figure it out by accident, or they don't figure it out at all.

"The artists and creators getting real results aren't just posting more—they're reaching the right people, at the right time, with the right message. Once you know how to do that, everything changes."

I built this to cut through that noise. I started with a simple idea: what if I looked at your channels, understood exactly why your audience isn't growing, and gave you a clear roadmap of what to do? No fluff. No vague strategy. No ongoing commitment unless you want one.

The results I've gotten for creatives—12,000 followers in 15 days, 2 Million+ total interactions in 30 days, a million accounts reached—didn't come from magic. They came from understanding where the right audience is, what makes them care, and how to build real connection.

I'm based in Las Vegas, but I work with creatives everywhere. The struggles are universal—confusing algorithms, declining reach, not knowing why your stuff isn't getting seen. The solutions, once you know what they are, tend to be simpler than you'd expect.

How we think

Three things we believe that most agencies don't.

01 — Honesty

The truth is more useful than comfort

We'd rather tell you something that's hard to hear than give you a polished report that doesn't change anything. If your content is bland, we'll say it. If you're on the wrong platform, you'll know. That's what you're paying for.

02 — Specificity

Generic advice is worth what you paid for it

We don't do templates. Every audit is built around your specific business, your specific audience, and your specific situation. The action list you get back is for you — not a version of you that represents a thousand other clients.

03 — Simplicity

Complexity is usually just confusion in disguise

The best social strategies aren't complicated. They're clear, consistent, and built around what your audience actually responds to. If you feel overwhelmed, that's a sign something's off — not a sign you need to do more.
