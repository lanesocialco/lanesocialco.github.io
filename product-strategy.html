How is this different from the Audit?
The Audit tells you what's broken RIGHT NOW on your existing profiles. The Strategy tells you where to go from here. You might have a perfect profile but no idea what to post or who to target. The Strategy answers all of that — it's your business plan for social media.
Do I need to have an existing presence?
No. The Strategy works whether you're starting from scratch or scaling existing profiles. We'll recommend the right platforms for your business and tell you exactly how to build your presence on them.
Can I get both the Audit and Strategy?
Absolutely. The Audit + Strategy Bundle gets you both at $150 — saving $44 versus buying them separately. You'll get the diagnosis of what's currently broken AND the roadmap for what to build next.
What happens after the strategy call?
You have a complete roadmap and detailed strategy document. You can implement it yourself, hire someone to manage it, or reach out to us if you want help executing the plan.
Is the 90-day roadmap realistic?
Yes. We build our roadmaps based on what actually works in your industry, not hype. The milestones are specific, achievable, and tracked. You'll know exactly what to expect.
