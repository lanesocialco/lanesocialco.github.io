Frequently Asked Questions

Why is the bundle cheaper than buying them separately?
Good question. When you buy the bundle, we do both audits and strategies more efficiently — there's overlap in the research and discovery process. We pass those savings on to you. It's a real discount, not fake FOMO pricing.
Do I have to do the audit first or can I do the strategy first?
They happen in parallel, not one after the other. Day 1 when you submit your intake form, we start the audit process. While that's happening, we start the strategy work too. By day 5 you get your audit. By day 7 you get your strategy and the review call.
Can I just buy one product instead?
Absolutely. The bundle is for people who want the complete picture. If you only need one piece, the Social Media Profile Audit and the Custom Social Media Strategy are both available individually for $97 each.
What if I already have an audit from someone else?
You can buy just the strategy. The strategy doesn't depend on the audit — you can do one, the other, or both. Just let us know if you already have an audit so we don't duplicate the work.
What happens after the 30-minute call?
You have two complete documents and a clear plan. You can implement everything yourself, hire a social media manager to execute it, or come back to us if you want hands-on implementation help.
