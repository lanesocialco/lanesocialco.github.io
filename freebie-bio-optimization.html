The anatomy of a perfect bio

Line 1
Your Value Prop

State what you do and for whom in 20 characters or less. Lead with words your audience searches.

Line 2
Specific Offer

Give a concrete benefit. Not "follow for tips" but "Get your free audit checklist" or "Schedule a call."

Line 3
Proof or Detail

Show who you serve, what outcome they get, or where you're based. Makes you credible and specific.

Link
Strategic Link

Lead to your highest-value offer: email signup, booking link, audit intake, or best content. Not your homepage.
